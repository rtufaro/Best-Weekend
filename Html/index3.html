Saturday Itinerary

Today is the Day!

I hope you had a restful night, today is going to be a big one! To start the day off right, brunch will be at Bruce & Son's. They have an amazing brunch menu with delicious cocktails. I highly recommend the Hotcake with a Mimosa. This gets you feeling right for the day ahead.

After you finished your lovely first meal, you are heading to Croteaux Vineyard. This place is paradise and althugh it only has Rosé, don't let this stop you from going if you are not a fan of Rosé. After you soaked in the greatness of this lovely vineyard the next stop is Kontokosta, another lovely vineyard. This vineyard has amazing cliffside views and is immaculate.

After you visted the two winery's the next stop is American Beech. This restaurant is back in the town of Greenport and is a lovely restaurant with great drinks and atmosphere. When you have finished there the next stop is The Times Vintage, you may be bloated but this vintage store is so fun to go into. It is a vintage store with a 70's essence that is so groovy. They have vintage records and acessories and homewear that are to die for.

When you're ready to head out from the groovy 70's it is time to head to Port Waterfront and Grill this is the pre-game bar before you head over to Claudio's. This restaurant sits right by the water and you can see the ferry to Shelter Island come in! Once you are done, take a quick walk on the board walk to Claudio's. On your way look at the yachts docked on the water they are insane and make sure to pick up your jaw before heading to Claudio's.

Claudio's is the last stop for the day, I hope you had a great day and Sunday will be very relaxing!
