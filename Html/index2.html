Friday Night Itinerary

You have Just Arrived, Get Ready for the Best Weekend!

Whether you took the Hampton Jitney or drove down, you had a long day but it's not time to end the day yet! Have a quick nap and then it is party time!

Before you head out for a night on the town it is essential that you have a bite to eat. Depending on what time you get to Greenport there are several lovely options depending on your food preference for the evening.

Places to go for your first night are Hellenic Snack Bar and Grill which is a short drive from the town of Greenport and they have the best Lemonade! If you are too tired to travel and want to stay in town there is noah's which is New American cusine, the Frisky Oyster which is an upscale restaurant with great drinks, Kontiki is a laid back and beautifully decorated European bar, and if you are in the mood for a plate of nachos and a margarita Lucharitos is the place for you!
