Sunday Itinerary

Today is Your Last Day :( We Have Some Sites to See Before You Leave!

Wow! Time really flies when you are having fun! Today is a relaxing day and to start you have to recover from your hangover with a bagel from Goldman's Bagels in town.

After you had your bagel its time to get a breath of fresh air! The first place to visit is Lavender by the Bay. They have a lovely lavender field to visit but be sure if you go to go at the end of June - beginning of July to see the lavender in full bloom! After you head over to the field it is time for the beach. Head to the 67 Steps Beach, the name gives it away as there are a lot of steps to get to the beach, but it is definetly worth it!
